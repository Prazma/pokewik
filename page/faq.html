What is PokeWik

PokeWik is your very own "pocket wiki" which you can add on anything that has to do with "writing online".

You can add your PokeWik on: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Google Services, Mail and so much more!

##How do I use it? You don't need the "fancy buttons" to edit your pocket wiki. You can write

Big Letters

Headers

Bold Letters

Links

Banners

With just symbols you can type with your keyboard easily.

Click "New Page" to get started at the editor
